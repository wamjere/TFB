The Father's Blessing Ministry: Men's Empowerment Gathering

  • 20th October 2025
  • 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • RCCG Solution Centre, Nairobi, Kenya

The Father's Blessing Ministry is a dedicated men's ministry within RCCG Solution Centre, Nairobi, Kenya, aimed at nurturing, empowering, and equipping men for leadership, spiritual growth, and purposeful living. Our gatherings provide opportunities for men to connect, share experiences, and strengthen their faith.

Event Highlights

  • Inspiring teaching and devotional sessions from seasoned leaders.
  • Practical workshops on leadership, family life, and personal development.
  • Interactive sessions for mentorship and networking with fellow men of faith.
  • Prayer and worship sessions focused on men’s spiritual growth.

"A man of faith is a man of purpose. At The Father's Blessing Ministry, we walk together to fulfill God’s calling for our lives."

Who Should Attend?

This event is open to all men who desire to grow spiritually, connect with like-minded individuals, and become agents of positive change within their families, workplaces, and communities.

Registration & Participation

Attendance is free, but prior registration is encouraged to help us plan and provide the best experience. Join us for a transformative experience and be blessed as we celebrate the calling of men to greatness in Christ.
